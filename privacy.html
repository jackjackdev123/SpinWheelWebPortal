Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 14, 2026. This policy describes how SpinWheel: Random Picker respects and protects your digital privacy.

Introduction

Welcome to SpinWheel: Random Picker (referred to as "the App" or "we"). We are committed to providing a reliable, fun, and completely secure decision-making tool. We strongly believe that your personal decisions, configurations, and list data are your own private business.

Because of this belief, we have architected SpinWheel to be a 100% local-first application. This Privacy Policy outlines what information the App uses and how we ensure your data never leaves your device.

1. Purely Local Operation

Unlike most decision-making apps, SpinWheel is designed to run entirely offline on your mobile device (Android & iOS). We do not operate any cloud servers to store your personal content, and we do not require you to register an account, log in, or provide any identity information.

Zero Cloud Storage Guarantee

All of your customized wheels, wheel options, custom weights, roll dice presets, coin flip history, random numbers, and name lists for shuffling are saved directly inside your device's secure local storage (e.g., local SQLite database/SharedPreferences). We have no tech stack that transfers this data onto external servers.

2. Data Collection and Usage

Personal Information

We do not collect, read, transmit, or store any sensitive personal identifiers such as your name, email address, physical location, device phone number, or network IP addresses.

Usage Data & Analytics

To deliver an uncompromised, privacy-first user experience, SpinWheel does not integrate any third-party marketing SDKs, trackers, or behavioral analytics software. We do not monitor how often you spin the wheel or what specific choices you make.

3. Third-Party Payments and In-App Billing

SpinWheel is a completely ad-free application. We offer a Pro Version Upgrade for an optional, one-time purchase ($3.99) to unlock premium functionalities (such as unlimited wheels, weight probabilities, bulk import, and list sorting).

All in-app billing operations are handled directly and securely by the respective operating system platforms:

  • Apple App Store In-App Purchases (for iOS users)
  • Google Play Billing Library (for Android users)

We do not collect or store your payment card details or financial billing addresses. When you buy Pro, the app receives a secure transaction receipt from Apple or Google to activate the features locally. These payment processes are regulated under Apple's and Google's individual privacy policies.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

Because your data is strictly stored locally, it remains on your device until you choose to delete it. You have total control over your data:

  • You can edit or delete individual wheels or presets at any time inside the App.
  • You can reset all databases to their default state by clicking "Clear History" or "Reset Data" in the settings panel.
  • Uninstalling the App from your device will automatically and permanently delete all local SQLite databases and saved items.
Warning Irretrievable Data Loss

Because we do not have a server database, we cannot back up or recover your data for you. Once you uninstall the App or clear its local storage cache, any custom lists, histories, or settings will be permanently lost and cannot be retrieved.

5. Children's Privacy

Our services do not target or collect data from anyone under the age of 13. Since we do not collect any personal data or implement trackers, SpinWheel is fully compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and General Data Privacy Regulations (GDPR) guidelines for young users.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time as we roll out updates or new features. We will notify you of any revisions by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed about our data handling practices.
